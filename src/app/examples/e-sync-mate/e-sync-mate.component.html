What is EDI Integration?

Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) is the backbone of modern business communication. It enables organizations to exchange essential documents such as purchase orders, invoices, and shipping notices in a standardized and automated format. EDI eliminates manual data entry, reduces paperwork, and minimizes errors, making it an indispensable tool for businesses of all sizes.
